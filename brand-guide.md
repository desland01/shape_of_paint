## 4) Messaging Pillars (USPs)

### 4.1 White-Glove Project Experience
Purpose-built systems designed to protect the home and the final result.

- AirCare System™: Multi-stage HEPA filtration removing 99.97% of airborne dust and contaminants.
- Soft-Landing Floor System™: Multi-layer protection engineered for luxury surfaces.
- Fully managed execution: shoe coverings, documented SOPs, end-of-day cleanup. Clients are never asked to supervise.

### 4.2 Zero-Surprise Guarantee
Predictability is designed into the project before work begins.

- Measured production-rate estimating.
- Locked pricing and defined change orders (priced and approved in writing).
- Scheduled, reliable delivery with defined arrival times.

### 4.3 Trusted With Premium Coating Systems
Expert-level application of advanced North American and European coatings.

- Manufacturer-trusted field expertise.
- International coating experience (North America, Italy, UK, Germany).
- Advanced finish capabilities built on chemistry knowledge.

### 4.4 Three Generations of Penner Craftsmanship
A family legacy of building and finishing homes since the 1950s, including landmark and heritage projects. Craft refined and passed through generations.

## 5) The Offer

### Outcome
A high-standard paint or coatings project designed to be predictable from start to finish.

### What Clients Receive
- Fixed pricing and locked timelines.
- A HEPA-clean, carefully controlled jobsite.
- Premium North American and European coating systems.
- Craftsmanship built on three generations of residential experience.
- A process that removes stress, uncertainty, and decision fatigue.

This is painting without the mental load.

## 6) Client Process (How It Works)

1. Brief discovery call: confirm fit, goals, and timing.
2. In-home consultation: detailed walkthrough, issue identification, clearly defined scope options.
3. Fixed written quote: exact price, exact scope, exact start and finish dates.
4. Guaranteed project slot: dedicated calendar placement; dates are not shuffled.
5. White-glove execution: scheduled arrivals, protected floors and air, daily updates.

## 7) Included Enhancements (Not Add-Ons)

- Colour guidance to reduce decision fatigue.
- Protection upgrade included on full-interior scopes.
- Post-project home reassembly.
- Labeled touch-up kit.
- Finish care guide (printed and digital).
- Finish samples for key rooms.

No add-ons. No nickel-and-diming. Just a complete experience.

## 8) Capacity and Scarcity

To protect finish quality and guaranteed timelines, only a limited number of projects are taken on each month. Once a month fills, new projects move to the next available window.

## 9) Investment Philosophy

This is not a square-footage quote. Investment is anchored to longevity, quality of execution, predictability, reduced disruption, healthier indoor air, and long-term peace of mind. This is a fixed price for a controlled outcome.

## 10) Scope Flexibility (Without Compromising Standards)

- Client-prep option.
- Phased approach.
- Alternate finish allocation.
- Flexible start window.
- Simplified colour palette.

All adjustments are discussed, priced clearly, and approved in advance.
