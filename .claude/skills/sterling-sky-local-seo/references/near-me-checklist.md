# "Near Me" Ranking Factors Checklist

Based on Sterling Sky's analysis of 8,186 businesses across 200 cities.

## Address Visibility

- [ ] **Show your address** (if you have a qualifying location)
- [ ] Address matches exactly across GBP, website, and all citations
- [ ] Have a real, staffed office (not virtual office or UPS store)
- [ ] Map pin correctly placed on Google Maps
- [ ] If SAB: evaluate whether showing address is worth the ranking benefit

**Testing tip:** If rankings drop after hiding address, test showing it again.

## Review Velocity (Most Important)

- [ ] Reviews coming in consistently each month
- [ ] Target: Match or exceed competitor review velocity
- [ ] Review generation process built into workflow
- [ ] Not relying on old reviews to carry you
- [ ] Tracking monthly review count

**Warning signs:**
- Gap of 2+ weeks without new reviews
- Competitors getting 3-5x more reviews/month

## Review Quality

- [ ] Encouraging text reviews (not just star ratings)
- [ ] Reviews mention specific services/products
- [ ] Reviews include location/neighborhood mentions
- [ ] Responding to ALL reviews within 48 hours
- [ ] Negative reviews addressed professionally

**Ideal review:** Contains text, mentions specific service, includes local reference.

## Review Rating

- [ ] Average rating above 4.0 (minimum)
- [ ] Ideally 4.5+ for competitive niches
- [ ] No sudden rating drops (investigate if so)
- [ ] Monitoring for fake negative reviews

## Landing Page Content

- [ ] Substantive content (not thin/filler)
- [ ] Service descriptions with real detail
- [ ] Location-specific content (neighborhoods, landmarks)
- [ ] Not keyword-stuffed (meaningful words only)
- [ ] Answers common customer questions

**Word count is not the goal.** Quality and relevance matter.

## Photos (Industry Dependent)

### Visual Industries (Restaurants, Salons, Photographers)
- [ ] Recent photos (within last 30 days)
- [ ] High-quality images
- [ ] Variety: exterior, interior, team, work samples
- [ ] Customer-uploaded photos (social proof)

### Service Industries (HVAC, Plumbing, Garage Doors)
- [ ] Basic photos present (logo, exterior, team)
- [ ] Don't over-invest in photo optimization
- [ ] Focus efforts on reviews instead

## Ranking Factor Priority

**Ranked by impact:**

1. **Address visibility** - Show it if you can
2. **Recent review velocity** - Consistent monthly reviews
3. **Review text content** - Encourage written reviews
4. **Rating average** - Keep above 4.0
5. **Landing page content** - Substantive, not thin
6. **Photos** - Industry dependent
