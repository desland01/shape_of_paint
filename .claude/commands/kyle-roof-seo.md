## Kyle Roof's Key Principles

### The Four Critical Placement Areas
Always optimize these four areas for target keywords:
1. **Title Tag** - Under 60 chars, front-load keywords
2. **H1 Tag** - One per page, include primary keyword
3. **URL** - Short, clean, keyword-included
4. **Body Content** - Natural density guided by TF-IDF

### TF-IDF Optimization
Use Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency to:
- Determine which keywords to include
- Calculate optimal frequency
- Identify missing related terms
- Avoid over or under-optimization

### Scientific Testing Methodology
1. **Single Variable Testing** - Change one factor at a time
2. **Control Groups** - Maintain unchanged test subjects
3. **Inverse Testing** - Confirm by both adding AND removing factors
4. **Wait 2-4 weeks** minimum before measuring results

### Key Findings from 400+ Tests
- Title tags matter more than most realize
- Word count is NOT a direct ranking factor
- Content relevance (TF-IDF) beats length
- Internal linking structure significantly impacts rankings
- Page speed has diminishing returns after baseline
